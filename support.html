Frequently Asked Questions

Starting a meditation routine can be simplified into three steps: finding a quiet space, setting a time, and choosing a meditation technique that suits you.

Yoga can enhance your body's flexibility, strength, and balance. It also promotes relaxation, mindfulness, and improved breathing.

Even a few minutes of daily meditation can make a big difference. You can start with 5-10 minutes per day and gradually increase the duration as you become more comfortable.

A yoga mat is helpful but not necessary. Comfortable clothing that allows free movement is more important. Props like blocks or straps can be useful but are optional.
