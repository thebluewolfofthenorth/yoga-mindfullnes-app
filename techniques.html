Yoga Techniques

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Stand with your feet together or hip-width apart, ground down through the four corners of your feet, and roll your shoulders away from your ears.

Cat and Cow Pose (Marjaiasana / Bitilasana)

These poses are performed together, providing a gentle massage to the spine and belly organs. They also help in warming up the body for further movement.

Ujjayi Breathing

Known as "victorious breath", this involves inhaling and exhaling through the nose while slightly constricting the back of the throat.

Mindfulness Techniques

Body Scan Meditation

Lie on your back with your legs extended and arms at your sides, palms facing up, and focus your attention slowly and deliberately on each part of your body.

Sitting Meditation

Sit comfortably with your back straight, feet flat on the floor, and hands in your lap, then focus your attention on your breath or a specific thought.

Observer Meditation

Detach from internal thoughts and observe them without judgment, recognizing them as they arise and pass without getting attached.

Mindful Breathing

Focus on your breath, observe the inhalation and exhalation, and bring your mind back whenever it wanders.
